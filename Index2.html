A Journey of Growth

Reflecting on Engagement & Development in the Course

Within My Comfort Zone

Connecting with Classmates

Engagement was enjoyable as it allowed me to connect with peers, share ideas, and see how others approached the same problems from different angles.

Learning from Diverse Perspectives

I learned the value of diverse viewpoints. Another student's insight would often make me rethink a concept, which was a powerful way to learn.

Where I Was Challenged

Refining Moodle Technical Skills

The development side really pushed me to stretch beyond my comfort zone. It was rewarding to see how much progress I made with the platform.

Unlearning Old Habits

It was a challenge to stop the mental comparison to other LMS systems. This forced me to be more adaptable and patient with the learning process.

What Could Have Overwhelmed Me

Learning the Platform vs. Content

At first, it felt like I was spending as much time learning the platform as I was engaging with the content, which was initially frustrating.

Navigating Moodle's Tools

Trying to figure out all the tools for assignments and activities could sometimes slow me down, testing my patience with the process.

The Power of Support

"Honestly, I can't really say that anything was overwhelming. The community was great, our 'mentors' provided insights along the way. I didn't feel that I couldn't finish."
